Experience Optimization in Social Networks

For static applications such as blogs, wikis, etc. there is explicit moderation via human intervention as well as implicit moderation based on keywords etc. No such moderation methods exist for dynamic group interactions such as with instant messaging. A method that enables maintaining a quality of experience in a dynamic group interaction environment based on social network characteristics is proposed.
