Hello, I'm John!


I am a Director of User Experience and Sr. Solutions Architect at Epsilon a subsidiary of Publicis Groupe. I have 12 granted utility patents and am the author of 3 books on digital prototyping.

With over a decade innovating in technology as well as working with heterogeneous client and server configurations (Unix / Linux, Windows, Mac OS, iOS, and Android), I've performed in various technology roles across multiple verticals spanning Telecommunications, Manufacturing, Consumer Package Goods, and Data Services. I have built numerous physical and digital prototypes to refine UI/UX and System innovations, communicate future concepts and empower users.

I also have a Masters Degree in Design Methods from The New Bauhaus at Illinois Tech Institute of Design.

Designing for an inclusive future!


From humble beginnings in rural Kentucky, I understand life's challenges. I am a living example that education and hard work can change one's station in life. I believe technology can be used for good and improve the lives of everyone. I deeply love my wife, children, and family and believe together we can foster an inclusive society. With empathy and innovation, I'm advancing this vision for our future.

Childhood explorations with console tube radios and semi-conductors inspired a love for computers and gaming (Atari Basic, 5200 and Nintendo), electronics and audio. I have hands-on experience with design and prototyping, manufacturing (testing for audio, vision systems, robotics and RF) as well as IP generation.

I look forward to bringing my passion and dedication to help your organization achieve its vision.
